On the basis of answers to your questions, you receive badges for being especially helpful.

Badges Description

   
🥇 Gold   -   Gold badges recognize important contributions from members of the community.

                         They are the highest in rank and awarded to those who had tried to give best possible answer.

                         This Bagde is awarded when the number of answers by a user is reached to 6 or more.
   
🥈 Silver   -   Silver badges are less common than bronze ones. You'll need to plan your strategy to get one of these.

                          This Bagde is awarded when the number of answers by a user is reached to 4 or more.
   
🥉 Bronze   -   Bronze badges encourage users to try out new features on the site. They are easy to get if you try!

                             This Bagde is awarded when the number of answers by a user is reached to 2 or more.
