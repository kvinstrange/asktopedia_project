    About Us

    Asktopedia is the web app in which all technical users can ask their doubts with other registered developers. Developers who post answers can also upload relative material and documents for further references. Our app gives various badges and appreciation rewards to developers once they earn certain badges.

    Services

    Asktopedia is an online platform that provides a variety of services for developers and programmers. Some of the key services provided by Asktopedia include:




    Q&A Community:

    Asktopedia is a Q&A community where developers and programmers can ask and answer technical questions related to software development, programming languages, tools, and frameworks.





    Providing Badges:

    Users of Asktopedia can earn "badges"; for example, a person answers to 50 various questions, can receive badges for their valued contributions, which represents a gamification of the traditional Q&A website.
