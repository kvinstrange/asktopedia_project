    About Us

    Asktopedia is the web app in which all technical users can ask their doubts with other registered developers. Developers who post answers can also upload relative material and docs for further references and also provide support by sessions and online meetings. Our app gives various badges and appreciation rewards to developers once they earn certain badges.
